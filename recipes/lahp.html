Beef Lahp

Description

Beef Lahp is a traditional Lao dish. Filled with a variety of herbs and spices, Beef Lahp consists of mint leaves, shallots, dried pepper flakes, rice powder, and much more. Each bite is packed with flavor from the fermented fish sauce and lime. This dish is usually eaten with sticky rice. A Lao staple food. It requires you to roll the rice into a little ball with your hands and you scoop the lahp onto the rice then eat it.

Ingredients

  • Sliced beef
  • Cilantro
  • Mint leaves
  • Shallots
  • Rice powder
  • Dried pepper flakes
  • Lime
  • Sugar
  • Fermented fish sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the sliced beef to your preferred doneness and add to mixing bowl
  2. Chop the cilantro, mint leaves, and shallots then add to mixing bowl
  3. Sprinkle rice powder, dried pepper flakes, and sugar into mixing bowl
  4. Squeeze one whole lime into mixing bowl
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of fermented fish sauce
  6. Mix all ingredients together and serve
